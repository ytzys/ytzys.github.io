物流系统派车单查询流程

登录流程

  1. mainPage 的地址的是 http://test.sditds.gov.cn:81/logistics/mainPage
  2. 直接请求的话会跳转到登录界面 https://test.sditds.gov.cn:5565/cas/login?service=http://test.sditds.gov.cn:81/logistics/security/login
  3. 访问 http://test.sditds.gov.cn:81/logistics/security/login 也会跳转到上面的地址

对于本地工程来说，访问 http://localhost:8080/logistics/mainPage
会跳转到 https://local.sditds.gov.cn:8443/cas/login?service=http://local.sditds.gov.cn/logistics/security/login

派车单查询请求接口

http://test.sditds.gov.cn:81/logistics/forwardingOrder/dispatch/listData?pageNum=1&pageSize=10&billNo=&forwarderName=&consigneeCName=&flowStatus=&delivTimeStart=&delivTimeEnd=&oriBack=&_=1533695610680
